# Airdrop

A snapshot of the Starknet mainnet state was taken on **March 26, 2026**. Eligible controllers can claim their free games directly on the game website. A controller may qualify under multiple categories—rewards stack, up to a **maximum of 3 free games** per airdrop.

| Criteria                                          | Free games |
| ------------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| Owned at least 1 EGS NFT before snapshot \*       | **1**      |
| Held at least 1 $NUMS (legacy) before snapshot \* | **1**      |
| Held at least 1 $LORDS before snapshot \*         | **1**      |
| Held at least 1 $PAPER before snapshot \*         | **1**      |
| Held at least 1 $FLIP before snapshot \*          | **1**      |

_\* Only Cartridge Controller accounts are eligible._

:::tip
**Stacking.** Rewards from different categories stack, up to a maximum of 3 free games. A controller that held $NUMS (legacy), $PAPER, and $LORDS would receive 1 + 1 + 1 = 3 free games. A controller that qualifies for all 5 categories would still receive 3 free games.
:::

:::warning
All airdrop claims must be made before **July 1, 2026 at 00:00 UTC**. Unclaimed free games after this date are forfeited. Once claimed, free games expire after **24 hours**.
:::

## Embedded Game Standard (EGS) NFT

- Controllers that have ever owned at least one Embedded Game Standard NFT before the snapshot are eligible.
- EGS is a token standard developed by [Provable Games](https://docs.provable.games/embeddable-game-standard) on Starknet mainnet. It provides a unified NFT interface for fully onchain games, allowing in-game assets to be represented as composable, interoperable tokens across the ecosystem.
- In practice, flagship titles from Provable Games such as [Loot Survivor](https://lootsurvivor.io) and [Summit](https://www.summit.game/) already rely on this standard. If you've played either of these games and minted or acquired any in-game NFT through a Cartridge Controller, you likely qualify under this criterion. More broadly, any game built on top of EGS is covered, so this category captures a wide swath of the most active onchain gamers on Starknet.

## NUMS (legacy) players

- Controllers that have ever held at least 1 $NUMS (legacy) before the snapshot are eligible.
- $NUMS (legacy) was used as a reward during the first version of the game and is distinct from the current NUMS token.

## Realms World players

- Controllers that have ever held at least 1 $LORDS before the snapshot are eligible.
- $LORDS is the token used in [Realms World](https://realms.world), a fully onchain game ecosystem on Starknet.

## Dopewars players

- Controllers that have ever held at least 1 $PAPER before the snapshot are eligible.
- $PAPER is the token used in [Dopewars](https://dopewars.game), a fully onchain game on Starknet.

## Flippy Flop players

- Controllers that have ever held at least 1 $FLIP before the snapshot are eligible.
- $FLIP is the token used in [Flippy Flop](https://flippyflop.gg), a fully onchain game on Starknet.
