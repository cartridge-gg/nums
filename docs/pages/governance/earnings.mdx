# Earnings

## Protocol Revenue

The protocol earns revenue from:

- **Game entries** — Entry fees paid by players
- **Protocol fee** — Share of each game entry (after burn, rewards, referrals)
- **Other sources** — As defined by governance (e.g., partnerships)

## Distribution

Protocol earnings can be allocated to:

- **Treasury** — Reserve and operational funds
- **Staking rewards** — Distributed to vault stakers
- **Burn** — Additional NUMS burn for deflation
- **Development** — Team and ecosystem grants

## Transparency

Earnings, allocations, and distributions are trackable on-chain. Governance proposals define how revenue is split and spent.
