# Treasury

## Overview

The **Treasury** centralizes protocol funds and can be controlled or influenced through governance.

## Treasury Functions

- **Holds protocol assets** — USDC, NUMS, or other approved tokens
- **Fund allocation** — Governance votes on spending
- **Operational reserve** — Supports protocol operations and development
- **Staking rewards** — Can allocate funds to vault stakers
- **Grants and partnerships** — Community-approved initiatives

## Control

Treasury actions typically require:

- A passed governance proposal
- Timelock delay for execution
- Multisig or controlled execution logic

Funds are only moved when governance approves specific proposals.
