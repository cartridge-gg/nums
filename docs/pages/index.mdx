Welcome to the official documentation of **the game**, a fully onchain game built with [Dojo Engine](https://dojoengine.org) on [Starknet](https://starknet.io).

## About

**The game** (Number Challenge) is a skill-based strategy game. The goal is to place 18 numbers into slots in ascending order—your placement decisions, use of power-ups, and ability to navigate traps determine your success.

## Core mechanics

- **Number range**: Each number is between **1 and 999**
- **Sequential placement**: You always see the current number and the next one; place each number into one of 18 slots when it's your turn
- **Ascending order**: All numbers must be placed in ascending order (equal numbers may be placed consecutively)
- **No rearrangement**: Once placed, a number cannot be moved or swapped
