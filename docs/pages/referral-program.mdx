# Referral program

The game offers a referral program: players who bring in new users can receive a share of entry fees.

## How it works

1. **Referral link** — Each user has a unique link to share
2. **Conversion** — When a new player purchases a starterpack via this link, the referrer is recorded
3. **Payment** — A portion of the amount paid is credited to the referrer

## Parameters

- **REFERRAL_FEE** — Percentage of the amount credited to the referrer
- **PROTOCOL_FEE** — Protocol fees deducted before calculating the referral share

## On-chain data

Referrals are recorded in claim/starterpack purchase events. The `referrer` field in events allows attributing payments to referrers.

## Interface

The **Referral** section in the application displays:

- Personal referral link
- Payment history received
- Amounts and dates of executed referrals
