# Economy

Players buy a game with **USDC**. The entry is distributed across several uses: most of it is used to buy back and burn the token, a smaller portion goes to the referral program when applicable, and the remainder flows to the protocol vault as revenue. When a player finishes a game, rewards in NUMS are newly minted based on their performance and the state of the economy.

## Reward Distribution

When the player finishes a game, rewards in NUMS are **newly minted** based on their score and their multiplier (see section below). They are credited immediately to the player's wallet—no manual action is required. Once received, NUMS are fully free to use: stake them, trade them, or hold them—no restrictions apply.

:::note
**Supply impact.** If the player underperforms, supply will have been burned in the process. If the player overperforms, supply will have been created in the process.
:::
