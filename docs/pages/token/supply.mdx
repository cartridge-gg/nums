## Estimated supply

An **estimated supply** is defined at the protocol level to determine the supply coefficient (see the corresponding section in [Economy](/token/economy)). This estimate is initially set at 1,000,000 at the time of this document and can be adjusted up or down by the DAO. This value enables, among other things, a maximum supply of approximately 2,000,000 tokens.

## Supply dynamics

Player activity on the game continuously **burns** and **mints** tokens. Over time, community performance is the only criterion that stabilizes the token supply—which remains between 0 and 2,000,000.

## Price

Although the USD value of the token over time is not known in advance, we can nonetheless estimate that its price will depend on protocol revenue. Since you must stake the token to receive a share of protocol revenue (see the [Staking](/staking) section for more details), it is likely—barring euphoria—that the price will be directly correlated to these revenues. The more the protocol is used, the more revenue it generates, and the more attractive it becomes to hold shares in the protocol to capture a portion of that revenue.

:::note
**Initial reference price:** $0.01 per NUMS (see the distribution above for details).
:::
